About Apphive

Apphive is an independent project building small, focused web apps that solve specific problems well. We believe most software is bloated, ad-heavy, and tries to do too much. Our apps do one thing, do it cleanly, and respect your time.

Everything we build is browser-based and mobile-friendly. There are no logins, no tracking pixels beyond standard analytics, and no subscriptions. Where we run ads (like Google AdSense on this site), it's to keep the apps free for everyone.

Built with

  • TypeScript and modern web standards
  • No frameworks where vanilla works fine
  • Static hosting — fast, secure, cheap
  • Open content where appropriate (e.g. Wikimedia Commons road sign artwork in TrafficNZ)

Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

Apphive itself does not collect personal information. We do not require accounts, and our apps store user data (such as quiz progress in TrafficNZ) in your own browser using localStorage — not on our servers.

Cookies and analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns. This involves first-party cookies that record anonymised information such as page views, browser type, and country. You can opt out via your browser settings or the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ads Settings or learn more about how ad networks use information at aboutads.info.

For visitors from the EU, UK, or other jurisdictions requiring consent for personalised advertising, your preferences are managed through Google's Funding Choices consent banner where applicable.

Third-party services

Our apps may load resources from third-party sources such as Wikimedia Commons (for road sign images in TrafficNZ). These services have their own privacy practices governed by their respective policies.

Children's privacy

Our apps are suitable for general audiences but are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your rights

Because we do not collect or store personal information on our servers, there is generally nothing to access, modify, or delete. Local data stored by our apps in your browser can be cleared at any time through your browser's settings.
